About ApolloCanis

ApolloCanis was founded by Chris Perko in May of 2014 with the release of Swarm Defense for Android, with Fruit Catch following it in the fall of the same year. What started off as a single-game Android project is now becoming a multi-platform gaming company.

Coming in 2015 - a brand new PC dark action game is in the works. Currently under the alias "Project Plagueland", ApolloCanis plans to open the game up for free to anyone interested in the early alpha versions. A paid version will be released at a later time.

Swarm Defense

This is a new twist on the old Atari game, Missile Command! Defend your base against the alien swarms! Shoot them all!

Fruit Catch

Catch as many fruits as you can! Fill up the buckets to add more time, but watch out for catching the wrong fruit in the wrong bucket!
