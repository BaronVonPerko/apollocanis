San Antonio Game Development

ApolloCanis was founded in San Antonio, TX by Chris Perko in May of 2014 with the release of Swarm Defense for Android, with Fruit Catch following it in the fall of the same year. What started off as a single-game Android project is now becoming a multi-platform gaming company.

Coming in 2015 - a brand new PC dark action game is in the works. Currently under the alias "Project Plagueland", ApolloCanis plans to open the game up for free to anyone interested in the early alpha versions. A paid version will be released at a later time.
